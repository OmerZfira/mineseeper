Don't Know The Rules?
The Rules:
1. Click to reveal a tile.
2. If you reveal a mine, the game ends.
3. If you reveal an empty square, you keep playing.
4. If you reveal a number, it tells you how many mines lay hidden in the eight surrounding squares.
5. Right-click to flag a tile as a suspected mine.

You win by flagging ALL and ONLY the mines!
